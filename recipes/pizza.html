Pizza

Description

Pizza is a flat, round Italian dish that's made of thin, leavened wheat-based dough. The dough is topped with tomatoes and cheese, and sometimes other ingredients, like anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, and meat. It's then baked in a very hot oven, typically in a commercial setting. Pizza is usually served hot.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Make your own dough.
  2. Keep the sauce and toppings simple.
  3. Bake it hot.
